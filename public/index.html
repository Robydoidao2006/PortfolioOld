About Me

Robert Fialho is a 27 years old man, who was born in Plantation Florida on October 19, 1990. When he was 3 years old, his parents got divorced, and he ended up moving with his dad and sister to Venezuela where he grew up. He managed to learn Portuguese at home, because his dad and stepmother spoke it all the time, and he learned Spanish in school. At the age of 14, his dad and stepmother got a divorce and they ended up moving to Brasil. He graduated from high school in Brasil in 2010.

He moved back to the United States. He ended up taking an intensive English course at Florida State University where he spent a year to learn the language. Three years later he moved to Portland Oregon to help his brother with his business. But that did not work, and he moved to Orlando Florida. He found out about a Full-stack Web Development program at UCF and that got his attention, and so he applied for the course in November 2017.
